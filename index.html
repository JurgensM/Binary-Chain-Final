Decentralized Audit : Main Characters

Hunters

Hunters are the core of our on chain security system, their role is to vote for new protocols to be deployed after conducting a paid audit on them. Hunters are chosen among the best seekers.

Seekers

A seeker's role is to help Hunters find potential breaches by commenting under contracts that are being audited in that moment. Seekers can earn and be promoted to Hunters through hackatons.

Detectives

Detectives' role is to periodically search for potential off-chain problems that could impact the integrity of the to-deploy or already deployed protocol and get a reward for their revisions.
